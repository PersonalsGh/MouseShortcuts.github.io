About Us

The story behind Mouse Shortcuts.

The Story

Mouse Shortcuts was built by a university student and solo founder who wanted a faster way to work on Windows — and couldn't find anything that did exactly what was needed. So they built it.

What started as a personal project quickly turned into something bigger. After sharing a demo video on Instagram that gained over 100K views, it became clear that other people wanted this too.

The Mission

Mouse Shortcuts exists to make your everyday computer use faster and more intuitive. We believe your mouse is an underutilized tool, and with the right software, it can do so much more than point and click.

Open Development

This website documents the entire journey — from building the app to marketing and monetizing it. We believe in transparency and want to help other indie developers build, ship, and grow their own products.

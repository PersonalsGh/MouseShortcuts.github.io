Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

MouseShortcuts ("the App") and its associated website are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how data is collected, used, and safeguarded across our software and web platforms.

2. Information Collection

Account Information: We collect your name and email address (via Google OAuth or Supabase). We do not collect addresses or phone numbers.

Advertising & Affiliate Data:

Website: We use Google Ads (and may transition to Ezoic) to serve advertisements. These services collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and cookies to personalize and measure ad performance.

App: The desktop app uses a "bridge" hosted on our domain to display affiliate marketing links and clickable images within a WebContentsView. Clicking these links may share basic referral data with affiliate partners (e.g., Amazon, VPN providers).

Data NOT Collected: We do NOT collect shortcut configurations (stored locally), keystrokes, mouse movements, clipboard contents, or screen data.

3. How Information is Used

To maintain your account, authenticate users, display advertisements on the website, serve affiliate content in the app, and communicate updates.

4. Advertising, Cookies & Affiliates

Google Ads: Served on our website using cookies for frequency capping and personalization.

App Content: We display affiliate links via clickable images in the desktop app. In the future, we may display direct sponsorship ads within the app.

Bridge Technology: The app connects to a bridge hosted on our domain to fetch and display this marketing content.

5. Third-Party Services

We utilize Supabase (Authentication/Database), Google Ads (Website monetization), and various Affiliate Programs (App monetization). We may switch to Firebase or other equivalent services in the future.

6. Data Retention & Rights

Data is kept while your account is active. You may request deletion via the App Settings; data is removed within 30 days. You have the right to access, correct, or object to the processing of your data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be effective immediately upon posting. Continued use of the App or website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

For privacy concerns: mouseshortcuts@gmail.com.
